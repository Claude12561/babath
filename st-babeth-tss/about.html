Who We Are

Founded in 2010, ST BABETH Technical Secondary School is dedicated to equipping students with hands-on technical skills and academic excellence. Located in Rutsiro District, Rwanda, we prepare learners for professional success in ICT, business, and applied sciences.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical and vocational education in Rwanda and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and training that promotes innovation, discipline, and employability among youth.
